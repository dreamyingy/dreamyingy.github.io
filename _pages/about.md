## Selected Course Research Projects

### Human Activity Recognition

Analyzed smartphone inertial-sensor data for six daily activities. Used t-SNE, PCA, clustering, logistic regression, and MLP models; logistic regression reached 96.4% test accuracy while highlighting generalization trade-offs.

### San Francisco Crime Classification

Built a data-science pipeline for 878K SFPD records, including data cleaning, spatial-temporal exploration, feature engineering, modeling, and interpretation of crime-category patterns.

### StoryDiffusion CSA Optimization

Improved StoryDiffusion Consistent Self-Attention by replacing pure random token sampling with a hybrid similarity/random strategy, aiming to preserve character details while maintaining visual diversity.

### Semantic Similarity for LLM Evaluation

Evaluated whether embedding-space similarity can predict LLM answer correctness. Compared short-form and long-form QA behavior and analyzed why fluent outputs may still be factually unsupported.

### Video Super-Resolution

Compared interpolation and SRCNN baselines, BasicVSR/IconVSR reproduction, and generative enhancement with ControlNet-Tile and Conditional Flow Matching, focusing on fidelity and temporal consistency.

### arXiv Research Briefing Agent

Developed a six-skill research agent for arXiv paper search, ranking, evidence extraction, network analysis, structured briefing, and grounded follow-up QA from cached artifacts.
